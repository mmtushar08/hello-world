FAQ

Common questions

Is Webflow right for our B2B SaaS company? +
Yes — Webflow is now the default choice for Series A–C SaaS companies that need editorial speed, visual quality, and engineering flexibility. It's ideal if your marketing team needs to update the site weekly without opening a Jira ticket.
How long does a Webflow build take? +
A standard Webflow marketing site (10–25 pages, full CMS, integrations) takes 5–8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Larger builds with e-commerce or complex logic take 8–14 weeks.
How do you achieve high Lighthouse scores on Webflow? +
We defer third-party scripts, lazy-load all images, optimise Lottie files, remove unused Webflow.js modules, and implement proper font loading. We audit every build before launch and target 90+ across all Lighthouse categories.
Can you handle migrations to Webflow? +
Yes. We handle SEO-safe migrations from WordPress, Squarespace, Framer, and HubSpot CMS to Webflow. This includes full redirect mapping, content migration scripts, and post-launch monitoring.
What does a Webflow project cost? +
A production Webflow site for a B2B SaaS company starts at $15,000. Full-scale builds with CMS, integrations, and migration run $30,000–$80,000. Ongoing retainers start at $3,500/month.
