FAQ

Common questions

Are you an official HubSpot partner? +
Yes. Takshvi is a certified HubSpot Solutions Partner. We work with HubSpot's partner team and have direct access to technical support, beta features, and the partner directory.
How long does a HubSpot CMS build take? +
A full build (custom theme + 15–30 modules + migration) typically takes 8–14 weeks depending on scope. Discovery and architecture take 2–3 weeks. Active build runs in 4–8-week sprints.
Can you migrate our existing website to HubSpot CMS? +
Yes. We handle full SEO-safe migrations from WordPress, Drupal, Webflow, Sitecore, and other CMS platforms — including full redirect mapping, content parity audit, and post-launch monitoring.
What's the difference between HubSpot CMS and Content Hub? +
HubSpot rebranded CMS Hub to Content Hub in 2023. Content Hub includes all CMS functionality plus AI content tools, brand voice settings, and content remix capabilities. We build on Content Hub and support all tiers.
What does a HubSpot CMS project cost? +
A custom HubSpot CMS website starts at $30,000 for a mid-size B2B site. Enterprise builds with complex personalisation and migration work run $60,000–$120,000. Ongoing retainers start at $4,500/month.
